Next Na’vi article will be in the New York Time Magazine

Posted on November 29, 2009 by matt

The next article on Na’vi (the language) will be in the New York Times Magazine by Ben Zimmer, sometime in early December, as I learned from an email to Paul Frommer, the creator of the Na’vi language.

The Ubisoft game will be released before the movie and will have Na’vi language in it.
