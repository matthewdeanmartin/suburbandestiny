Calendars and Technology

Posted on September 15, 2007 by matt

So this morning I get up at 9 AM & think, what to do today?  Well I check meetup.com and I see the coffee meet-up at 10 AM. D’oh! That’s in 10 minutes. I miss it.

About 1 PM I am still dinking around the house and see the Vegan meetup is having a meetup at 2PM. D’oh! I just at lunch at noon.

At 4 PM I see ACE had a stream clean up this morning. D’oh! Missed it by six hours.

Will I make it to a movie? At this rate, I think the day is jinxed.

Moral of the story, assemble your calendar a month in advance.  At least the laundry is getting done.
