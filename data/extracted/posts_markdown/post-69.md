This wasn’t exactly a good monday.  When you are able to start counting the negative reactions you’ve gotten in one day from various people (I’m up to four–hmm, seems like more) one starts to wonder if one is doing something wrong or if it is just a bad run of luck to bump into a bunch of cranky people one right after another.


Or maybe it is because I’m sick today and I’m just hearing the negative in what people have to say.  Nah, people are pretty cranky today.  Must be some sort of mood syzygy.


Anyhow, I’m going to go crawl under my rock and rest.  If anyone has a problem with it, they can reach me by sending a message to [dev.nul](http://web.ukonline.co.uk/eric.price/humour/hum0156.htm).
