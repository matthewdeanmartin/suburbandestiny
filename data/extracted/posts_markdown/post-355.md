title: Reset Vista’s GUI to XP

Why this does exist as a single step operation, I don’t know. XP introduced themes so that people could personalize their computer. Microsoft does seem to understand that I never wanted to have a flash GUI for my work machine. I want boring!


1\. [Replace Vista’s copy command](http://www.robgonda.com/blog/index.cfm/2007/8/6/Vista-File-Copy-Blows--Seek-Alternative). Download [TeraCopy](http://www.codesector.com/download.php).


2\. Disable [UAC](http://www.petri.co.il/disable_uac_in_windows_vista.htm).


3\. Disable [Aero](http://www.howtogeek.com/howto/windows-vista/disable-aero-on-windows-vista/), set classic [theme, start menu and taskbar](http://www.theeldergeek.com/windows_classic_start_menu.htm).


4\. Set [folder view to classic](http://windowshelp.microsoft.com/Windows/en-US/help/13d46a66-24a5-47d0-be06-47649ccfa85b1033.mspx).


5\. [Fix System Restore Size](http://www.howtogeek.com/howto/windows-vista/reduce-system-restores-disk-usage-in-vista/) (which grows to fill up you harddrive under normal usage).
