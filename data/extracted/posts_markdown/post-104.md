I’ve had it for over a month now and I used it for (roughly in order of importance and frequency):  

1\) MP3 \& audio books (while driving and at the gym)  

2\) Ebooks (especially when on vacation, but sometimes at home or standing in line)  

3\) Password management  

4\) Flashlight (the screen is almost bright enough to illuminate a small area)  

5\) Time of day  

6\) Phone number (but only once, since my cell has all the numbers I’ve ever needed to call)  

7\) Family photos, used for showing off


Some still unused features include:  

Bluetooth  

Infrared  

Voice notes


Underused:  

Writing down anything  

Todo list  

Calendar  

Games  

Recipes (but that is because I’ve hardly been cooking, not because I’ve reverted to paper recipes)
