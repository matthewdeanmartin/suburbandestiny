What’s up with McCain supporters going around and [smashing people’s windows and stealing their yard signs](http://www.flickr.com/photos/ivy_windchaser/2939110809/)?


Just more evidence, that there isn’t a conservative party in the US, just [creaping fascism](http://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/content/article/2008/03/01/AR2008030101539.html).  The consequences of a two party system is you get to vote for people who have [no respect for law](http://www.salon.com/news/feature/2008/10/22/voter_supression_guide/) and no [respect for law and order](http://www.wavy.com/Global/story.asp?S=9211532&nav=23ii).  Or you get to vote for a party that might disagree with you on your favorite issue.  Or you can stay home.


Without law and order, without the rule of law, abortion and who is more pro\-gun is the [least of our concerns](http://www.worldbank.org/html/prddr/trans/so97/albania2.htm).
