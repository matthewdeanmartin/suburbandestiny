One year ago, my coworker’s son [Paul Balint Jr. died in action](http://www.community-news.com/node/829) in [Ramadi, Iraq](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ramadi). Paul served on our behalf, doing a job that someone had to do.


![](https://suburbandestiny.com/tech/wp-content/uploads/2007/12/121707-1531-heroespaulb1.png)


In memory of Paul Balint, Jr, let reciprocate his service and learn what we can do to serve the 100,000\+ troops serving in harm’s way in Iraq.
