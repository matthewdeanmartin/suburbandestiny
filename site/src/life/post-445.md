Fantastic movie.  Everyone should see it ask themselves, which party, given that we only have two, is likely be backbone of the Norsefire party in the US?  Which party would create [fear and war](http://www.vanityfair.com/politics/features/2006/07/yellowcake200607) to [remove our freedoms](http://www.lewrockwell.com/gregory/gregory10.html)? Which party will use [fear of the other](http://www.politicalarticles.net/blog/2007/07/06/racist-jim-crow-prince-william-county-virginia-targets-immigrants/) to make obey what we would never obey if we were afraid? (In our own back yard of Virginia no less)  We have only one thing to fear, and it isn’t the fear– it is that we vote people who would manipulate the fear of everyone around us.


I’m not afraid, and besides, inspiring true fear takes a certain amount of [competence](http://blogs.abcnews.com/politicalpunch/2008/09/another-aip-off.html).


Everyone planning to vote should watch this movie.
