title: Tragedy and Reaction

There is a meme that sez “Bad things happen, let’s not do anything about it”


No fine tuning of laws. No government reaction. No search for explanations. Bad things happen\-end of story.


While “Don’t do something, just sit there!” is a funny Buddhist joke, when applied to matters of public policy, it’s counter productive.
