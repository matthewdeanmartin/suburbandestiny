title: Icelandic without an Icelandic Keyboard

All special characters lose their hats, split the æ, ð becomes d, þ becomes th.


ö – o  

á é í ó ú – a e i o u  

æ – ae  

ð, þ \-d, th


I first saw the technique [here](http://kristinsnae.blog.is/blog/kristinsnae/entry/584252/).
